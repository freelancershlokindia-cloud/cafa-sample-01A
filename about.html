Born in the heart of Ranchi, Jharkhand, THE SAMPLE ONE started with a simple vision: to bring world-class, premium coffee culture to our vibrant city. We believe that coffee is more than just a drink; it's an experience. Our beans are ethically sourced, roasted to perfection, and brewed by award-winning baristas.
